Tri-Tip

Garlic Soy Sauce Tri-Tip

This recipe takes a little bit of a different direction than typical Tri-Tip recipes. This recipe will involve cutting the Tri-Tip into smaller steaks which are then seasoned indivually this leads to very flavorful and juicy steaks.

Ingredients

  • Whole Tri-tip (about 2 lbs.)
  • Garlic Powder
  • Bottle Of Soy Sauce
  • Butter
  • Splash of Beer(Optional)

Steps

  1. Use a butcher's knife to cut the Tri-Tip along the grain into pieces around 1" thick. (they will be different lengths because of the shape of the roast)
  2. Place steaks into a Cake pan and pour one bottle of soy sauce onto steaks and into pan.
  3. Sprinkle a generous amount of garlic powder over the steaks and into the pan. (You may now add a splash of your favorite beer if you would like)
  4. Let the steaks marinate for around 20 minutes before removing from pan and placing on the grill.
  5. Get a good sear for around 2 minutes on each side and then proceed to grill them to your desired level of doneness.
  6. Top steaks off with a small amount of butter on top while still fresh off the grill.
  7. Ready to serve!!
