Spaghetti with Meat

This is a classic easy to make dish that will also produce leftovers for a smaller family.

Ingredients

  • Angel Hair Pasta noodles
  • 1 lb Ground Beef or Ground Turkey
  • 1 can of Prego Traditional Pasta Sauce
  • 1 Table spoon of sugar(Optional)

Steps

  1. In a skillet, brown the meat and drain fat
  2. While meat is cooking, being to boil a large pot of water
  3. Once meat is browned, add pasta sauce and mix in, setting the heat to a low simmer
  4. Cook Spagetti noodles as indicated on the packaging
  5. If desired, sprinkle a tablespoon of sugar into finished pasta sauce
